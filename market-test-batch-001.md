## Manual Re-Check Steps

Before any role moves into issue #13 execution:

1. Re-open the source link and confirm the ad is still live.
2. Confirm remote policy: worldwide, country-only, region-only, or hybrid.
3. Confirm work authorization and payroll/contracting feasibility.
4. Confirm timezone overlap from Thailand.
5. Confirm seniority expectations: delivery ownership, mentoring, architecture, people leadership, interview process.
6. Confirm stack requirements and non-negotiables.
7. Identify the top 5 repeated words from the ad for CV/message tailoring.
8. Save a PDF/screenshot or local note if the role looks actionable.
9. Choose application path: direct application, recruiter message, hiring-manager message, or warm intro.
10. Only then update `application-tracker.md` during issue #13.

If the ad is stale or infeasible, mark it as `Closed / expired` or `Paused` in issue #13 only if Richard has intentionally saved it in the tracker. Otherwise leave it as a batch note.
