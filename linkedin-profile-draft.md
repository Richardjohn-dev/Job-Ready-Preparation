## About Section

I’m a product-oriented software engineer with strong .NET experience across integration-heavy SaaS, document/data workflows, and background-job systems. I’m backend-leaning overall, but I’m also comfortable shipping product-facing work in Vue and Nuxt when the product needs it.

Much of my work has followed the same pattern: taking messy business workflows, external APIs, and operational edge cases, then turning them into configurable systems people can actually run. That has included document and metadata movement, admin tooling, background processing, workflow automation, and the visibility needed to understand what a system is doing when it runs in production.

A large part of that experience came through RDrive DataBridge, where I built integration workflows connecting RDrive with platforms including Aconex, Azure Blob Storage, Autodesk, and Asite. More recently, RefineBridge has become the modern-stack example of the same kind of work: .NET, Nuxt/Vue, PostgreSQL, auth boundaries, billing and webhooks, integrations, background jobs, and operational clarity.

I’m mainly targeting software engineering roles where .NET is central and product/integration work matters, while still keeping room for longer-term product building. The common thread is building reliable workflow software that connects systems, reduces manual work, and stays understandable when real operations begin.
