# Outreach Scripts

Current as of 2026-06-01.

Purpose: support a small market test with concise, honest outreach. Use these to start conversations, not to mass-message people.

## Positioning Anchor

Use this as the base:

> Backend-leaning .NET engineer with full-stack Vue/Nuxt capability, focused on SaaS workflows, integrations, background jobs, and operationally visible systems.

Safe Snagr/RDrive wording:

> Long-term independent developer/contractor for Snagr/RDrive, owning integration-heavy product work across document/data workflows and external platforms.

Do not claim:

- Formal employment status unless confirmed.
- SharePoint or 4Projects implementation.
- Sales, marketing, revenue, adoption, or user-count ownership.
- Raw client names, private screenshots, live URLs, IDs, or credentials.

## Anti-patterns To Avoid

- Do not apologize for the non-standard Snagr/RDrive arrangement.
- Do not overexplain career history in the first message.
- Do not send generic "I am passionate" copy.
- Do not claim seniority louder than the evidence.
- Do not ask strangers for broad mentorship; ask one specific question.
- Do not attach private screenshots or raw RDrive evidence.

- Whether to say "remote from Thailand", "UK/Europe overlap", or another phrase.
- Work authorization by region.
- Salary/rate bands.
